GOSPEL VAN


The Kurichi church ministry is among the poor but we are strong in faith. We are praying for the surrounding villages which have not yet been reached with the Gospel of Jesus Christ. The surrounding village people are bound by community rituals, idol worship, poverty, illiteracy, and spiritual darkness. I kindly request you to pray for these people that they may open their hearts towards Christ Jesus. Prayer Brings Victory. We need a Van for our church evangelism team members to reach these villages to serve the Lord. If you have a burden for perishing souls, you can make a donation for this vehicle which will be an encouragement to the Gospel team.

Thank you.

Yours sincerely,
the Lord’s humble servant,
Wilson Bruce. A.

ESTIMATION OF THE PROJECT

Dear Beloved in Christ,

In the premise of the church we are constructing the Toilet and Bath rooms for the church congregation as well as for the guest. In the days to come we may have bible training classes and conventional meetings. The utility of the Restrooms will be given to all the participant according to the time to time’s need. With this long time vision the construction plan is also improved. At present the construction work reached up to the level of roofing. The septic tank work, toilet basins purchase, plastering with tails, plumbing and roofing work, flooring work, footpath work, painting work, Electric work, Door work and outside plastering work are the major parts to be completed.

Thank you.

Yours sincerely,
the Lord’s humble servant,
Wilson Bruce. A.



The total expense for the completion of this construction is Rupees Four Lakhs fifty one thousand only. For our eyes this amount will be huge Number. But our God who created this Heaven and earth is a possible for all the impossible of humanity. Our God is expecting us to contribute with genericity towards His Kingdoms edification. Reward will be added in to your account in Heaven.

For the administration of this service not only supplieth the want of the saints, but is abundant also by many thanksgivings unto God; Whiles by the experiment of this ministration they glorify God for your professed subjection unto the gospel of Christ, and for your liberal distribution unto them, and unto all men; And by their prayer for you, which long after you for the exceeding grace of God in you. Thanks be unto God for his unspeakable gift. 2 Corn:9:12-15.

GOD BLESS YOU
