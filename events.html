COMMITTEE MEETING

Planning the Future: New Cathedral Discussion at Mettur Dam - August 12, 2023

Previous Next

A significant meeting was convened by Pastor Wilson Bruce and our committee, drawing together by the peaceful shores of Mettur Dam. Against the backdrop of this tranquil setting, a pivotal conversation unfolded concerning our new cathedral project. Pastor Bruce's vision of seamlessly blending tradition and modernity in the cathedral's design was shared, encompassing soaring spires and intricate stained glass,encapsulating the essence of our faith.
Following our productive meeting, a shared meal was enjoyed,deepening the bonds of camaraderie as food and stories were exchanged,all set against the breathtaking panorama of Mettur Dam. With a unified commitment and a funding strategy encompassing donations,events,and grants,the transformation of this collective vision into reality is underway.
The unfolding journey ahead promises to be one of unity and faith, enriched by the serene beauty of Mettur Dam.



EXCURSION

Journey of Faith and Unity: Pastor Wilson Bruce and the Youth Team Excursion - July 28, 2023.

Previous Next

On Friday, July 28, 2023, Pastor Reverend Wilson Bruce eagerly led the church's vibrant youth team on an exhilarating excursion to a serene nature reserve. The day was filled with laughter, camaraderie, and a deep connection with God's creation. As they embarked on this memorable journey, they shared reflections, engaged in heartfelt prayers, and participated in meaningful team-building activities that strengthened their bonds as a tight-knit community. With hearts overflowing with gratitude and cherished memories, they returned home, united and inspired to carry the light of faith and love into the world.
